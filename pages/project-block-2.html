Lalasia

An E-commerce landing page for high-end furniture

Lalasia offers a convenient app where you can browse a wide selection of high-quality furniture and home decor, and find your perfect match!.

Lalasia was created as a crossover between creative development and ecommerce, aiming to bridge the gap between the two facets that have traditionally not blended well together.

Role

As part of my responsibilities, I defined the project scope, managed the project, introduced animations, integrated the Firebase backend, and deployed the finished application to Vercel.

Methods

Functional Programming, System Design, Information Architecture,Testing

Tools

ReactJs, Material UI, GSAP, Vercel.

Challenge

The purpose of this project was to create a functional and visually appealing frontend that would assist users in managing their cryptocurrency assets.

Solution

I launched a project that aimed to investigate the boundaries between creative and practical development.
